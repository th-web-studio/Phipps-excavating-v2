Professionalism in Every Yard of Dirt

At Phipps Excavating & Land Management, we bring over 20 years of hands-on expertise to every project. We specialize in high-quality excavator services, including land clearing, dirt work, brush cutting, and complex drainage solutions. Based in West Liberty, we proudly serve Morgan County and the surrounding Eastern Kentucky region.

What sets us apart is our relentless commitment to precision and detail. We understand that excavation is more than just moving dirt. It is about preparing a foundation for the future. Whether we are reclaiming overgrown acreage or repairing a critical driveway, our workmanship is defined by efficiency, reliability, and results that meet the unique needs of every client.

Leadership & Community

Owner and operator Jamie Phipps is deeply rooted in the community. He brings the same dedication to his business that he provides to his public service roles:

  • Chief, West Liberty Fire Department
  • Member, White Oak Volunteer Fire Department
  • Member, Highlands Masonic Lodge

When you hire Phipps Excavating, you are working with a neighbor committed to the integrity and safety of our community.
